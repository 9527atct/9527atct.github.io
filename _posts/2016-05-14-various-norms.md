### vector norms ###
-------
Given a vector $x=(x_1,...,x_n)^T$. Then we defined the vector norms as follow.
- $ \| \| x \| \|_0 $ = the number of non-zero members of the vector.
- $ \| \| x \| \|_1 $ = $\| x_1 \| + ... + \| x_n \|$.
- $ \| \| x \| \|_2 $ = \sqrt{ \| x_1 \|^2 + ... + \| x_n \|^2 }
- $ \| \| x \| \|_{ \infty} $ =$ \max \left[ \|x_1 \|,..., \| x_n \| \right]$
- $ \| \| x \| \|_p$  = ( \| x_1 \|^p,..., \|x_n \|^p)^{ \frac{1}{p}}
